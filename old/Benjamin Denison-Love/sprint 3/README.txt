Application Description:

Our cooking-themed website aims to be the go-to platform for home cooks of all skill levels, offering a diverse range of recipes and instructional guides, with minor community engagement features. The platform provides a centralized hub where users can easily access valuable cooking resources and enhance their culinary skills.

Hypothesis:

I hypothesize that by providing a user-friendly interface and a regularly updating comprehensive resources, we can attract and retain a steady user base of cooking enthusiasts. The cards are designed to address various aspects of user interaction and engagement, ranging from recipe discovery and personalized recommendations to community participation and feedback gathering. Each card serves as a tool to implement and test different elements of our platform, aiding us in the iterative optimization process to better serve our users' needs.
