One-pan Garlic Mushroom Rice Bake

Quick rice dish with garlic, soy sauce, and mushrooms.
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Allergens: yeast, celery, soybean
Cooking Skill Level: Intermediate
Cuisine Type: Asian
Other Categories: Main Dish, Mushroom, Baking

Ingredients:
- vegetable oil: 2 tbs
- mushrooms, sliced: 375g
- rice vinegar: 2 tbs
- ABC Sweet Soy Sauce: 1 1/2 tbs, plus extra to serve
- brown onion, finely chopped: 1 small
- garlic cloves, crushed: 2
- 90-second white long grain rice: 2 x 250g pkts
- vegetable stock cube: 1, crumbled
- boiling water: 500ml (2 cups)
- frozen peas: 120g (3/4 cup)
- Fresh coriander sprigs: to serve

Instructions:
1. Heat half of the vegetable oil in a large flameproof baking dish over medium-high heat.
2. Cook the sliced mushrooms in the heated oil, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until just starting to soften.
3. Add rice vinegar and 1 1/2 tablespoons of ABC Sweet Soy Sauce to the mushrooms. Cook for 1 minute or until mushrooms are just tender.
4. Transfer the cooked mushrooms and sauce to a heatproof bowl. Set aside.
5. Heat the remaining vegetable oil in the same dish.
6. Cook the finely chopped onion in the heated oil for 2-3 minutes or until starting to soften.
7. Add crushed garlic and 90-second white long grain rice to the dish. Cook for 30 seconds or until fragrant.
8. Combine crumbled vegetable stock cube and boiling water in a medium jug. Add to the rice mixture along with the remaining sweet soy sauce. Stir to combine.
9. Add frozen peas to the dish. Bring to the boil, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes or until the sauce thickens and rice starts to catch on the bottom of the dish.
10. Stir in half of the cooked mushrooms (excluding the sauce) into the rice mixture.
11. Preheat the grill on high heat.
12. Spoon the remaining mushrooms and sauce over the top of the rice mixture.
13. Grill the dish for 3-4 minutes or until the mushrooms start to char slightly on the edges.
14. Serve the rice bake drizzled with extra sweet soy sauce and sprinkled with fresh coriander sprigs.

Cheesy Meatballs and Mushrooms

Cheesy French onion meatballs & mushrooms: A cozy winter meal.
Prep Time: 45 minutes
Allergens: gluten, wheat, egg, milk
Cooking Skill Level: Advanced
Cuisine Type: Australian
Other Categories: Main Dish, Mushroom, Beef

Ingredients:
- Egg (lightly whisked): 1
- Coles Beef 3 Star Regular Mince: 500g
- Panko breadcrumbs: 50g (1 cup)
- French onion soup mix (packet): 40g
- Colby cheese (cut into small pieces): 80g
- Vegetable oil: 1 tbsp
- Portobello mushrooms (thickly sliced): 200g
- Tomato paste: 2 tbsp
- Thickened cream: 125ml (1‚ÅÑ2 cup)
- Baby spinach: 60g
- Crusty bread slices: 4

Instructions:
1. Combine 1 egg (lightly whisked), 500g Coles Beef 3 Star Regular Mince, 50g (1 cup) panko breadcrumbs, and 1 & 1/2 tbsp French onion soup mix in a large bowl. Use your hands to mix evenly.
2. Roll a slightly heaped tablespoonful of the mixture into a ball, then flatten slightly. Place a small piece (about 4g each) of Colby cheese in the center of the flattened ball. Enclose the cheese and reshape the mixture firmly into a ball. Repeat with the remaining mixture and cheese. Place on a tray and transfer to the fridge for 30 minutes to chill.
3. Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Add half of the meatballs. Cook, turning occasionally, for 5 minutes or until browned evenly. Transfer to a plate. Repeat with remaining meatballs.
4. Add 200g portobello mushrooms, thickly sliced, to the pan. Spread out to a single layer and cook, without turning, for 3 minutes or until golden brown underneath. Turn and cook on the other side for 2 minutes. Transfer to the plate with meatballs.
5. Reduce heat to medium. Add 2 tbsp tomato paste to the pan and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Place 125ml (1/2 cup) thickened cream and 125ml (1/2 cup) water in a jug. Stir through the remaining soup mix until combined. Gradually pour the cream mixture into the pan, stirring until combined.
6. Return the meatballs and mushrooms to the pan. Cover with a lid and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 3 minutes. Add 60g baby spinach. Cover and simmer for 2 minutes or until wilted.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with crusty bread slices.
